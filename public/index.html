METHODOLOGY

How the Signal Score works

Tape Reader doesn't summarize headlines for casual reading — it ranks them for actionability. Every story is evaluated against market reaction patterns, speed of dissemination, expected move size, and shelf life, then compressed into a single Signal Score from 0 to 100 so you know in seconds which catalysts deserve your capital.

01

News In

Raw headlines ingested from primary sources the moment they hit the wire.

02

AI Analysis

Language models parse entity, sentiment, policy language, and surprise factor.

03

Historical Matching

Catalyst is matched against thousands of prior analogs by type, magnitude, and context.

04

Score Out

Signal Score 0–100 surfaces alongside sub-metric bars for immediate decision support.

SUB-METRICS
VELOCITY

Measures how quickly the market is likely to price the story — a proxy for urgency and immediate flow pressure.

MAGNITUDE

Estimates the expected move size if the catalyst is real — calibrated against analog distributions and current IV environment.

DECAY

Measures how fast the edge fades — high-decay catalysts demand immediate execution; low-decay stories allow for positioning over hours or sessions.

CONFIDENCE

Reflects agreement across analogs, source quality, and signal consistency — the system's internal conviction that the score is reliable.

FAQ

Common questions before launch

When does access start?

Tape Reader is pre-launch. Waitlist signups receive access in the order they registered, with Pro and Desk members prioritised. We'll notify you by email before opening the gates.

Which asset classes are covered first?

US equities and ETFs at launch, with coverage expanding to options flow, macro data releases, and crypto catalysts in subsequent releases based on waitlist demand.

Are scores delayed on Free?

Yes. Free-tier Signal Scores are delayed by 15 minutes. For intraday traders where catalyst timing matters, Pro delivers scores in real time as articles are processed.

Can I cancel later?

Billing hasn't started yet — there's nothing to cancel. Once live, all paid plans are month-to-month with no lock-in. Cancel any time before your next renewal cycle.

Will broker execution be supported?

Not at launch. Tape Reader surfaces signal intelligence — execution routing is outside scope for now. Broker integrations may be explored post-launch based on Desk-tier feedback.

What does API access include?

Desk-tier API access exposes raw Signal Score data, sub-metric values (Velocity, Magnitude, Decay, Confidence), and historical analog results in JSON format for programmatic use in your own workflows.

How far back do analogs go?

The historical analog engine currently indexes events back to 2010, covering major macro cycles, earnings seasons, and geopolitical catalysts. Coverage depth increases with Desk-tier custom analog requests.

Is this investment advice?

No. Tape Reader is an informational tool that quantifies news actionability. Signal Scores are not buy or sell recommendations. All trading decisions remain yours — manage your risk accordingly.
